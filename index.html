ğŸ§¾Classic (Kadaif) Recipe

Ingredients

  • Kadaif 250g
  • Pistachio paste 210g
  • White couverture 50g
  • Marshmallow 200g
  • Unsalted butter 45g
  • Cocoa powder
  • Cooking oil (for gloves)

Steps

  1. Melt butter with white couverture, mix in pistachio paste, and freeze 30+ minutes until firm.
  2. Toast kadaif over low heat until golden.
  3. Cut the filling into bite-sized pieces and oil your gloves.
  4. Gently melt marshmallow, then mix in cocoa powder.
  5. Spread marshmallow, wrap around the filling, and shape.
  6. Coat with cocoa powder and chill 5â€“10 minutes.
